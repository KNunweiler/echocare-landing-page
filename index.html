EchoCare provides a comforting, AI-powered voice companion for individuals with dementia, giving caregivers the respite they deserve and patients the reassurance they need.

Designed for the Reality of Caregiving

Cloned Voice Companion

Using state-of-the-art AI, we create a perfect replica of your voice, providing a familiar and comforting presence for your loved one during repetitive calls.

Caregiver Respite

Our service intercepts anxiety-driven calls, freeing you from emotional fatigue and allowing you to focus on high-quality, present interactions with your loved one.

Safety & Oversight

Review call transcripts and receive alerts for keywords indicating distress, ensuring you are always connected and aware, even when the AI is assisting.

Built on a Foundation of Trust & Ethics

Our use of "therapeutic deception" is guided by the core principles of bioethics and radical transparency with caregivers.

Beneficence (Do Good)

Our primary goal is to reduce patient anxiety and distress, providing a net positive benefit to their well-being.

Non-maleficence (Do No Harm)

We employ robust safety protocols and AI monitoring to prevent confusion and escalate potential emergencies.

Dignity & Respect

Every interaction is designed to be respectful, patient, and loving, upholding the dignity of the individual at all times.
